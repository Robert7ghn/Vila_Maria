    About us:

        Vila Maria, located in the Busteni mountain resort, only 50 meters away from Busteni Train Station and 500 meters to the Kalinderu ski slopes, has 5 rooms with a total accommodation capacity for 11 people. The rooms are equipped with TV, cable, private bathroom, free WI-FI, some rooms have their own balcony. A fully equipped kitchen is available for guests to prepare their meals, there are also facilities for barbecue located in the inner courtyard. The nearest grocery store and several restaurants are within a minute's walk. Vila Maria also offers free access to the private indoor parking.





Services:

Vila Maria, offers accommodation services in the mountain resort of Busteni, all over during the year, the location offering an imposing panoramic view over the Mountains Bucegi, of where you can admire every clear night, the illuminated Cross of the Heroes, from the mountain Caraiman.


Facilities:

The rooms are detached, furnished and properly equipped with a closet, bedside tables and beds, each with its own bathroom, color TV, Digi cable, double glazed windows, vertical curtains, hair dryer - on request.

The total accommodation capacity is 11 people.
